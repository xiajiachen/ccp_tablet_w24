Pan Seared Steak

Ingredients

  • 1 New York Strip Steak
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Butter

Instructions

Pat the steak dry and season with salt and pepper. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Add the steak and cook until desired doneness. Add garlic and butter in the last 2 minutes of cooking.

Hamburgers

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Onion
  • Cheese

Instructions

  • Combine the ingredients in a bowl.
  • Make four patties.
  • Grill the patties until the juices run clear and the meat is no longer pink in the center
